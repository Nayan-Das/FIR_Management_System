About Online FIR System


Tradition since 2019

A first information report (F.I.R.) is a written document prepared by police organizations in countries including India, Bangladesh, Pakistan etc. when they receive information about the commission of a cognisable offence, or in Singapore when the police receives information about any criminal offence.

We propose that the FIR be put on the blockchain to make the FIRs immutable. The parties involved will be accused, victim, Police and Judiciary. Whenever a document is submitted by the parties it can be verified by other parties. Ownership of change in any document will be transparent, i.e., if any one makes a change in FIR or submitted documents, the other parties can see who did that.
